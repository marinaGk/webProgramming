Meals for Members and Relatives

Our restaurant offers all its members and their relatives excellent dining options throughout the day. Our staff has a lot of years of experience and works really hard to make sure every meal at the club is a memorable one. Come here of you want to enjoy our great meals while watching a tennis game or a tournamnent.

OPENING HOURS:
• Monday-Friday 8:00am-7:30pm

In our restaurant you will find authentic and traditional delicious recipes such as wonderful salads, appetizers, homemade dishes, grilled meats, seafood, sweet desserts, ice creams, a wide variety of wine labels and other types of alcohol.

Our restaurant receives daily excellent reviews from its customers regarding the meals, the service and the overall experience they had from their visit.

Pay a visit to try our delicious meals and you will not be disappointed!
