TENNIS LESSONS

Courses for Members ┃ VILLIA TENNIS CLUB

JUNIOR CLASS

Our club provides junior courses for children over 4 years old. During these lessons, we make sure that the children are having fun and, at the same time, they are training and improving their abilities. The coaches of these departments are specially trained so that they can manage young ages with consistency and make the lesson enjoyable. Classes last about one to two hours and take place twice a week. Children and/or parents can, in collaboration with the coaches, determine the weekly schedule of the lessons.

JUNIOR CLASS SUBSCRIPTION:
• price / month: 25 €
• price / 3 months: 65 €
• price / 6 months: 140 €
• price / year: 280 €

ADULT CLASS

For adults we have special training courses, as well as experienced coaches who are prepared to train our members, even if they have never played tennis. Each member can take classes for three to four hours twice a week and the training schedule can be set by the members in collaboration with our coaches and staff. The adult sections are divided into three groups, depending on the student's experience in the sport. Group 1 is the the beginner's group, where you can choose if it's your first time playing tennis or you have played very few times. Group 2 is for people who have been involved in the sport for a short period of time and want to gain more experience. Group 3 consists of members who play tennis for a long time and their purpose is to gain experience at a professional level. The team in which each member is classified is determined by the coaches, after consulting with the trainees.

ADULT CLASS SUBSCRIPTION:
• price / month: 30 €
• price / 3 months: 80 €
• price / 6 months: 170 €
• price / year: 340 €

INTENSIVE COURSES

For our members who want to spend more time in the sport and gain a lot of experience in a short period of time this is the best option. In these courses, each trainee has the option of taking private lessons with one of our coaches for 2 to 3 hours every five to six times a week, or even every day. The trainee does not need to have any previous experience with tennis, as he can learn what is needed during the courses.

INTENSIVE COURSES SUBSCRIPTION:
• price / month: 55 €
• price / 3 months: 145 €
• price / 6 months: 290 €
• price / year: 585 €

PRE - COMPETITION COURSES

The competition courses are open to all those who want to take part in tennis matches and need to practice properly. For these courses we have specially trained coaches, who will train the contestants as much as necessary, will inform them properly about the competiton process and will guide them on the road to victory. The schedule of the training is determined by the trainee in consultation with his coach and is typically set to contain about three hours of training each day, four to five days a week. Prior to the competitions, the competitor will train intensively and, on the competition day, they will participate accompanied by their coach.
