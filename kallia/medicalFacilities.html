Our Medical Services

Located within the Resort, the Villia Health and Fitness Centre provides you with its expertise for your physical preparation and recovery, thanks to cutting-edge tools and amenities.

We brought together certified healthcare professionals so our members can enjoy optimum care.

Hot and cold baths for rehabilitation and recovery, physiotherapists, osteopaths, sports physician, indoor pool. We have all the facilities needed for the medical care and supervision of our tennis players.

In case of injury, our facilities and healthcare professionals will provide the best medical care possible.

Our medical facilities remain open as long as the club is open, in order to always provide medical care to our members.
