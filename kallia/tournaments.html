THE SCHEDULE OF OUR TOURNAMENTS

Our club organizes tennis tournaments at regular intervals, which take place at our facilities. All our members, regardless of age, experience or skill level, can participate in our tournaments, as well as people who are not members of our club but only want to participate in them (non-members usually have access to open tournaments only). To enter a tournament, you must fill in a form you will be given and write down the title of the one you want to participate in. In order to take part in one of our tournaments, firstly you have to register on our website.

You will find the full calendar of our tournaments at the bottom of this page.

Tinsel Tennis Tournament Summer 2022

In this tournament we will combine fancy dressing and tennis! Contestants are invited to attend the tennis matches wearing their most impressive costumes. Players are paired together, so there is no need to enter with a partner. Entry is open to adult men and women members and includes the tournament, lunch and a glass of wine. Also, the best dressed contestant will be awarded!

Waves for Wishes Open Tournament 2022

Waves for Wishes is a charity tournament open to both members and non-members of all ages. Competitors will play a ‘waves’ format with our coaches feeding the balls and explaining the rules. We also run a ‘Beat the Coach’ session at the end of the day. More information on the subject (both the ‘waves’ format and the ‘Beat the Coach’ session) will be provided during the tournament.

Club Closed Tournament and Finals Day

Every year we look for the best players of the club. The tournament is club closed, so articipants must be members of the club. every member of the club can participate in the tournament, regardless of experience and skill level.
